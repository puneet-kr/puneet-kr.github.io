Puneet Kumar

I am a Ph.D. student at Machine Intelligence Lab, Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, India, advised by Prof. R. Balasubramanian. My research interest includes Multimodal Emotion Analysis using Image, Text and Speech data.

I studied B.E. (Computer Science) from Manipal University, Karnataka, India and M.E. (Computer Science) from Thapar University, Punjab, India. I have worked at Oracle IDC Hyderabad as Software Engineer, Samsung R&D as Visiting Researcher and Osaka Prefecture University as Visiting Researcher. My detailed resume can be found below.
